# Privacy Policy for Harmonica Hero

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-31

**Harmonica Hero** ("we," "our," or "us") is an open-source educational game designed to teach users how to play the harmonica. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Harmonica Hero.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Microphone Data
Harmonica Hero requires access to your device's **microphone**.
*   **Purpose**: The microphone is used solely to detect the pitch of the notes you play on your harmonica in real-time. This allows the game to provide feedback on your performance.
*   **Processing**: All audio processing happens **locally on your device**.
*   **No Storage or Transmission**: We **do not** record, store, transmit, or share your audio data with any servers or third parties. The raw audio data is discarded immediately after the pitch is calculated.

## 2. Personal Data
We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that collect user data.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
